Chickpea Tikka Masala

Fragrant Spice-Infused Chickpea Tikka Masala: A Wholesome Indian Delight

Indulge in the vibrant flavors of this Spicy Chickpea Tikka Masala Delight. This delectable plant-based dish features tender chickpeas simmered in a rich, aromatic tomato-based sauce infused with a blend of exotic spices. The succulent tikka masala sauce, with its harmonious medley of flavors, creates a delightful balance of tangy, creamy, and spicy notes. Served over fragrant basmati rice or accompanied by warm naan bread, this hearty and satisfying recipe is sure to tantalize your taste buds while providing a wholesome and nutritious dining experience. Dive into a world of Indian-inspired culinary delight with this vibrant and enticing dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 red onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 can of tomatoes (or 4 fresh tomatoes)
  • 1 Tbsp paprika
  • 1 Tbsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cans of chickpeas, drained
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 1/2 lime

Steps

  1. Dice onions and garlic. (Cut tomatoes if using fresh)
  2. Heatoli in a pan over medium high heat, add the onion and garilc. Fry for 2 minutes.
  3. Add spices and tomatoe paste. Fry until fragrant and tomatoe paste is carmelised.
  4. Add the tomatoes and cook until soft and broken down (~5 min).
  5. Remove from heat and transfer to a blender, blend until smooth. If you are in a rush this step can be skipped.
  6. Add the blended mixture back to the pan. Add drained chickpes.
  7. Add the coconute cream and cook to let the cream and chickpeas soften (5 minutes)
  8. Serve with rice or naan.
