Chickpea Tikka Masala

More Descriptive Tile

Desribe recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 red onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 can of tomatoes (or 4 fresh tomatoes)
  • 1 Tbsp paprika
  • 1 Tbsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cans of chickpeas, drained
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 1/2 lime

Steps

  1. Dice onions and garlic. (Cut tomatoes if using fresh)
  2. Heatoli in a pan over medium high heat, add the onion and garilc. Fry for 2 minutes.
  3. Add spices and tomatoe paste. Fry until fragrant and tomatoe paste is carmelised.
