Chickpea Tikka Masala

More Descriptive Tile

Desribe recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 red onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 can of tomatoes (or 4 fresh tomatoes)
  • 1 Tbsp paprika
  • 1 Tbsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cans of chickpeas, drained
  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 1/2 lime

Steps

  1. Dice onions and garlic. (Cut tomatoes if using fresh)
  2. Heatoli in a pan over medium high heat, add the onion and garilc. Fry for 2 minutes.
  3. Add spices and tomatoe paste. Fry until fragrant and tomatoe paste is carmelised.
  4. Add the tomatoes and cook until soft and broken down (~5 min).
  5. Remove from heat and transfer to a blender, blend until smooth. If you are in a rush this step can be skipped.
  6. Add the blended mixture back to the pan. Add drained chickpes.
  7. Add the coconute cream and cook to let the cream and chickpeas soften (5 minutes)
  8. Serve with rice or naan.
