Creamy Overnight Oats with Sweet Berries

Indulge in the creamy goodness of these luscious Peanut Butter Overnight Oats. This irresistible breakfast treat combines hearty oats, velvety milk, tangy Greek yogurt, a touch of honey, and a generous dollop of smooth peanut butter. The flavors meld overnight, resulting in a luxurious and satisfying texture that will keep you energized throughout the day. Topped with a medley of sweet and juicy berries, each spoonful becomes a burst of vibrant flavors that perfectly complement the nutty undertones. Whether enjoyed as a grab-and-go meal or savored leisurely, these Creamy Peanut Butter Overnight Oats are a delightful way to kickstart your mornings with a wholesome and delicious twist.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 C Oats (Old Fashion Oats preffered)
  • 1/2 C Milk of choice
  • 1/2 C Yogurt
  • 1-2 Tbsp Peanut Butter
  • 1-2 Tbsp Honey
  • 1-2 Tbsp Chia Seeds
  • 1 Tbsp Cocoa Powder (or flavor of choice)
  • 1/4-1/2 Cup fruit (frozen or fresh)

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients except fruit in mason jar or container of choice. Stir to combine. Then add fruit and give a short stir to incorportate. (Leaving the fruit until last makes mixing the ingredients easier.)
  2. Cover tightly and put the container into the refrigerator. Eat within 6 days.
  3. Have fun with the flavoring and fruit combos!
