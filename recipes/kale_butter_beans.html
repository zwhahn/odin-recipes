Kale Butter Beans

More Descriptive Tile

Desribe recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches of kale, stems removed
  • 2 large leeks
  • 3 tins butterbeans
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup soaked cashews
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 2 tbsp white miso
  • 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Blanch kale: place kale in a pot of boiling water for 2 minutes, then plunge into cold ice water to stop the cooking
  2. Blend green sauce: Blend kale, cashews, nutritional yeast, 1 Tbsp olive oil, miso, 2 cloves of garlic, and juice of the lemon
  3. Cook leeks: Carmelise leeks in a medium heat with a big pinch of salt for 10 minutes until they're becoming brown and sweet. Add the rest of the garlic (minced) and the spices, cook for 3-4 minutes. Finally, add the butterbeans
  4. Add green sauce: Stir through the green sauce and bubble for 2 minutes. Take off the heat
  5. Finish: Finish with chili flakes, black pepper, olive oil and optional tahini yogurt sauce (1 Tbsp tahini, 2 Tbsp yogurt, lemon juice, water to loosen)
  6. Serve with bread/toast
